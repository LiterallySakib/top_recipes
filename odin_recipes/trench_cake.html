trench cake

Ingredients

  1. flour
  2. margarine
  3. brown sugar
  4. currants
  5. baking soda
  6. vinegar
  7. cocoa
  8. water

Instructions

Mix dry and wet separately, combine, bake until firm.

Significance

Created during WWI so families could send cakes to soldiers without spoilage. Eggs and milk were scarce, so vinegar and baking soda provided the rise. The cake was both a morale boost and a reminder of home.
